# TUI Airways | Roblox — Support Information Knowledge Base

## 1. Organisation

**TUI Airways | Roblox** is a fan-made Roblox Ro-Aviation airline based on the real-world TUI Airways UK division.

The group focuses on:

- realistic airline and airport operations;
- flight deck, cabin crew, ground handling, safety, and customer-service roleplay;
- an enjoyable experience for passengers and staff;
- a safe, inclusive, respectful, and moderated community.

The group is **not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to TUI Airways, TUI Group, or any real TUI company**. It cannot assist with real-world bookings, refunds, holidays, employment, or customer service.

## 2. Community standards

Users are expected to behave respectfully.

The following are not accepted:

- discrimination;
- harassment;
- threats;
- abuse;
- behaviour that makes the community unsafe;
- serious disruption of flights, training, or support services.

Discrimination involving gender, identity, sexual orientation, race, ethnicity, religion, nationality, disability, or age is treated seriously and may result in a community blacklist.

Moderation consequences are normally decided case by case. Serious rulebook offences may result in immediate termination, suspension, removal, or blacklisting.

## 4. Passenger information

### Flights

Flight times, routes, aircraft, and joining instructions are published in the Flight Updates channel.

### Passenger devices

Passengers may attend flights using mobile devices. Staff device restrictions do not apply to passengers.

### Suspicious-account restrictions

An account may be restricted where it has:

- fewer than one Roblox friend;
- membership in fewer than one Roblox group;
- an account age below 30 days.

These checks are used to reduce exploiting and disruption. Incorrect restrictions can be reviewed by Support.

### Special Assistance

Special Assistance is available where trained personnel are available.

Conditions:

- it is not guaranteed on every flight;
- booking is normally offered through the Flight Updates channel;
- a maximum of four passengers may receive the service on each flight;
- spaces are allocated first come, first served;
- only the practical assistance required needs to be provided.

### School or organised group visits

A group enquiry requires:

- Discord invitation;
- Roblox group link;
- expected number of students or participants;
- expected number of coordinating staff;
- preferred time or event.

Acceptance and flight availability are not guaranteed.

## 5. Passenger upgrades and server boosting

### Fast Track

Includes:

- priority boarding;
- fast-track security.

### Family Fast Track

Includes:

- priority boarding;
- fast-track security;
- access for up to three friends on the same flight at no extra charge to those friends.

### Server Booster benefits

Benefits include:

- Fast Track while the boost remains active;
- 300 Robux per boost;
- a maximum of three Robux boost rewards per month;
- an exclusive Server Booster role.

### Robux claim process

The booster provides a valid Roblox gamepass link.

The gamepass should:

- belong to a published Roblox experience;
- have the experience Maturity Questionnaire completed;
- be priced to provide the intended amount after Roblox marketplace tax.

Reward amounts:

- one boost: 300 Robux;
- two boosts: 600 Robux.

Ownership may take up to 48 hours to review and purchase the gamepass. Roblox may hold the Robux as pending for up to seven days. The payment should normally appear as pending shortly after purchase.

A shirt link may be reviewed in exceptional cases, but a gamepass is the standard method.

Fast Track is collected from a staff member during a flight and remains available only while the server boost is active.

## 7. Airport retail roleplay

### Costa Coffee or Starbucks

Recommended setup:

- relevant shop uniform;
- tag set to `Barista`.

Typical service sequence:

1. Welcome the passenger.
2. Take the order.
3. State the price.
4. Ask whether payment is by cash or card.
5. Roleplay preparing the order where appropriate.
6. Hand over the order.
7. Wish the passenger an enjoyable flight.

Recommended setup:

- Airport Shop uniform;
- white lanyard;
- tag set to the shop name or `Check-out Assistant`.

1. Welcome the passenger and confirm their purchase.

3. Ask whether payment is by cash or card.

5. Thank the passenger and wish them an enjoyable flight.

## 8. Staff applications

### General eligibility

Applicants are normally expected to:

- be at least 13 years old;
- comply with Discord Terms of Service;
- be verified as 13+ on Roblox;
- have unrestricted access to Roblox and Discord;
- belong to the official TUI Airways Roblox community;
- communicate fluently in spoken and written English;
- not hold a staff role in another TUI Airways recreation;
- attend at least two flights per week after joining staff;
- have a supported device;
- have a clear, working microphone where required;
- follow staff policies and training procedures;
- complete the training programme.

### Supported staff devices

Accepted setups:

- PC or laptop;
- Xbox or PlayStation with a connected keyboard and mouse.

Unsupported setups:

- touch-only phones;
- touch-only tablets;
- unsupported iPad setups;
- VR headsets;
- consoles without a keyboard and mouse.

### Voice chat

Operational employees are generally required to speak and listen in voice chat.

Reasonable adjustments may be considered where needed. Public Relations and Development roles may have different voice requirements.

### Applying

Applications and available roles are published through the Careers channel.

General applications aim to be reviewed within 72 hours, but this is not guaranteed.

Repeatedly asking for an early review may cause an application to be voided or declined.

### Application results

Results are normally sent by direct message from the TUI Careers bot, identified as **@TUI | Careers#9460** or `<@1336420935565185065>`.

Applicants should:

- enable direct messages from server members;
- remain in the main Discord server;
- provide the correct Discord ID, Discord username, and Roblox username;
- check the application-reading schedule in Careers.

A result may fail to arrive because of:

- an incorrect Discord ID;
- an incorrect Discord username;
- an incorrect Roblox username;
- leaving the main server;
- an unverified Discord account;
- disabled direct messages;
- a Discord or bot error.

Where no result has been received after seven days, a support ticket can be opened for a manual check. Missing-result cases are handled by Training & Recruitment, normally within 48 hours.

### Unsuccessful applications

Possible reasons include:

- not meeting a minimum sentence requirement;
- insufficient detail;
- answers that appear AI-generated or unnatural;
- incorrect or poorly explained competency answers;
- an unsupported device;
- using lists where paragraphs were required;
- poor spelling, punctuation, or grammar;
- not having a working microphone for a role that requires one;
- incomplete answers;
- repeated wording;
- duplicate applications;
- unsuitable behaviour or history;
- failure to meet a role-specific requirement.

An unsuccessful applicant may apply for another open department if they meet its requirements. Acceptance is not guaranteed.

### Recruitment days

Recruitment days are held when required and do not follow a fixed public schedule. Announcements are posted in Careers.

### Ramp Agent Fast Track or Ground Operations direct entry

This process may be offered for Ramp Agent or Ground Operations entry.

Information requested may include:

- Discord username;
- Discord ID;
- Roblox username;
- device used;
- whether the person applied within the previous 14 days;
- whether they currently work for TUI;
- whether they previously worked for TUI;
- microphone access;
- in-game chat access;
- previous experience;
- additional questions.

This route:

- applies only to Ramp Agent or Ground Operations entry;
- does not guarantee acceptance;
- may send results through the Careers bot;
- may only be available when announced;
- may require later applicants to use the normal Staff Interest Form;
- normally provides one Academy invitation.

## 10. Academy and training

### Standard Terminal Operations pathway

The general training pathway is:

1. STO Practical;
2. STO Theory;
3. department-specific training;
4. staff onboarding.

### STO Practical

The practical covers:

- theory slides;
- practical assessment;
- systems used across departments.

It is requested through the private `sto-practical-requests` channel. An examiner assists when available.

The practical normally lasts approximately one hour and fifteen minutes, although the duration may vary.

Official training is free. Offering Robux or another benefit for private training may result in a staff and user blacklist.

### STO Theory

STO Theory becomes available after the practical has been completed.

### Department-specific training

After STO Practical and STO Theory, trainees complete the training required for their selected department. This may include theory, practical work, or both.

### Onboarding

After completing department training, the trainee is inducted into the staff team and receives operational information.

### SSP and GOI terminology

SSP, GOI, and SSP Theory are legacy or case-specific training terms that may still appear in tickets, bot commands, or trainee records.

SSP-related issues are handled by Training & Recruitment.

Legacy SSP information includes:

- an 80% theory pass mark;
- another attempt may be granted after failure;
- after a successful remark, the result may need to be requested from the bot again.

The 80% pass mark does not automatically apply to STO.

### Training support

Training problems are handled by Training & Recruitment, normally within 48 hours.

### Trainee leave of absence

A trainee LOA request requires:

- duration;
- reason.

The trainee may be removed from the Academy server and given a message to retain as proof. On return, the trainee reopens a ticket and provides the proof.

Where total training time exceeds 21 days, the trainee may have to repeat the full training pathway.

### General leave of absence requirements

An LOA requires:

- a valid reason;
- the exact period of absence, including when it begins and ends;
- a duration proportionate to the reason.

False information may result in rejection and further action.

## 11. Sub-certifications, transfers, and reapplication

### Sub-certification

A request normally requires:

- Roblox username;
- Discord username;
- main department;
- requested sub-department;
- existing sub-certifications.

Restrictions:

- a maximum of two sub-certifications;
- department leadership members are not normally eligible;
- the person must normally have been fully trained staff for at least 14 days.

After approval:

- the Academy Helper may send a direct message;
- training may need to be completed within seven days;
- STO normally does not need to be repeated;
- the person proceeds directly to department training.

### Department transfer

A transfer request normally requires:

- Roblox username;
- Discord username;
- current department and qualifications;
- requested department;
- a genuine reason for transferring.

Conditions:

- the person must normally have been fully trained staff for at least 14 days;
- trainees normally complete their existing training before transferring;
- a trainee may instead resign, wait 14 days, and apply for another department;
- department-specific retraining may be required;
- retraining may need to be completed within 14 days;
- a transfer may be declined;
- an approved transfer is described as irreversible;
- a previous qualification may be retained where applicable;
- review may take up to 48 hours.

### Reapplication after leaving or failing training

The normal waiting period is 14 days after:

- resignation;
- termination;
- failed training.

A staff blacklist applies to the person rather than one account. It cannot be bypassed using an alternate account. Reapplication is possible only after the blacklist is formally removed or successfully appealed.

## 13. Staff rules

### Professional conduct

Staff must:

- use appropriate spelling, punctuation, and grammar during TUI operations;
- communicate respectfully and professionally;
- hide administrative commands from passengers by using the command bar, `/e`, or whispers;
- avoid advertising unrelated groups or services;
- avoid actions that jeopardise the Roblox group or Discord server;
- follow Roblox and Discord Terms of Service;
- avoid threats, abuse, and discrimination.

### Infractions and verbal warnings

- Infraction disputes are handled through a ticket.
- Infractions must not be discussed with other staff members.
- Hinting for promotion may result in a strike.
- Verbal warnings are normally informal reminders rather than formal infractions.
- Verbal warnings normally cannot be appealed unless issued in error.
- A verbal warning does not normally affect disciplinary status or promotion history unless the behaviour is repeated or escalates.

### Flight and training conduct

Staff must:

- follow reasonable instructions from department managers, the flight host, and management;
- remain professional during flights and training;
- avoid reckless driving, command misuse, system abuse, and disruption;
- give full attention during debrief;
- wait until debrief has fully ended before using tools, commands, or unrelated systems.

Failure to follow a reasonable management instruction may result in suspension.

### Sensitive or enforcement roleplay

Immigration, detention, or enforcement roleplay requires authorisation from Senior Management or Human Resources.

Staff must:

- end or de-escalate unrealistic or disproportionate scenarios;
- avoid prolonged or unjustified passenger restrictions;
- use enforcement tools only where reasonable and authorised;
- never roleplay bomb threats, terrorism, hijacking, drugs, blades, severe attacks, or similar incidents.

### Attendance and flight logging

- Staff must attend at least two flights per week unless covered by LOA or Reduced Activity.
- A flight may be logged only after attending the full flight.
- The logging command is `/flight_log`.
- A person who leaves early cannot log the flight.
- Forging or abusing flight logs may result in immediate termination.
- Staff who can no longer attend after reacting must tell their Department Manager at least 30 minutes beforehand.
- Logging too early may cause the log to be removed.
- Repeated early logging may result in a strike.
- Exact permitted logging times are contained in the operational guidelines.

### Command usage

Staff must:

- avoid commands that harm or disrupt passengers;
- avoid unrealistic commands unless authorised;
- target the username rather than `me`;
- normally type at least six letters of the username, preferably the full username;
- ensure restrictive commands are justified, proportionate, and time-limited.

### AFK

Staff must give their Department Manager adequate notice before going AFK.

### Confidentiality and ownership

Leaking, copying, stealing, or sharing TUI manuals, exams, documents, assets, or systems may result in termination and further action.

Material created for TUI is treated as owned by TUI Airways | Roblox unless another agreement exists.

### Department boundaries

Staff must not use another department’s tools without the required training and authority.

Examples include Cabin Crew using handcuffs or tasers without Safety & Security training.

### Termination and appeals

Senior Management may terminate a staff member.

A termination may be appealed through the helpdesk or to a more senior member. Submission of an appeal does not guarantee acceptance.

HR outcomes remain final unless Senior Management approves a formal appeal.

### Trainees

- Trainees hold temporary staff status.
- Trainees may be restricted to one role.
- Trainees may be limited to two request-related user pings per day, subject to context.
- A trainee may be removed for unsuitable devices, speed, competency, spelling, grammar, or conduct.

### Conflict of interest

Staff must not work for or own another Roblox recreation of TUI.

This is covered by Rule `1.14.1`.

### Self-roles

Staff must not assign roles to themselves, especially main-department roles.

### Soundboards

Soundboards are prohibited during flights and training. Repeated or distressing use may result in a strike.

### Voice-chat conduct

Staff must not:

- speak over others during important operations;
- abuse mute, deafen, move, or other voice permissions;
- enter voice channels to scream or disrupt operations.

### Rulebook acceptance

Holding a trainee or staff role, remaining in the Careers server, or participating in staff operations is treated as acceptance of the Staff Rulebook.

Continued participation after a rule update is treated as acceptance of the revised rules.

## 14. Human Resources investigations

An HR investigation may begin following:

- a report;
- a complaint;
- an appeal;
- an instruction from Senior Management.

Not every report becomes a formal investigation.

Investigations are handled by authorised HR personnel without a conflict of interest. A staff member may request a different agent where there is a reasonable concern about bias.

During an investigation, staff may be required to:

- attend a voice or written interview;
- respond within a reasonable time;
- behave professionally;
- answer truthfully and fully;
- provide screenshots, recordings, logs, or statements;
- keep the investigation confidential;
- avoid retaliation and witness interference.

Failure to cooperate may result in disciplinary action.

Temporary measures may include:

- suspension;
- role restrictions;
- removal from flights or operations.

Temporary measures do not automatically indicate guilt.

HR may withhold evidence, reasoning, or outcomes where confidentiality and data protection require it.

## 16. Resignation, reinstatement, and leave

### Resignation

Required information:

- Roblox username;
- Discord username;
- current role and qualifications.

Resignations are handled through Human Resources or staff administration.

A 14-day waiting period applies before reapplication.

### Reinstatement

Reinstatement may be considered only in exceptional cases.

Conditions:

- it is not guaranteed;
- approval is unlikely;
- the normal route is reapplication through the Staff Interest process;
- a previous or preferred role is not guaranteed;
- the request may be declined;
- retraining may be required;
- STO or department refresher training may be required before returning to operational duties.

A reinstatement review may require:

- Roblox username;
- Discord username;
- resignation or termination information;
- termination reason where applicable;
- previous roles.

Review may take up to 48 hours.

### LOA extension or amendment

Required information:

- Discord username;
- Roblox username;
- reason;
- when the absence begins;
- when the absence ends.

Changing an existing LOA period may require the old request to be removed and a new request submitted.

Ending an LOA early requires staff review.

On return, staff should:

- unmute relevant TUI servers and channels;
- resume reacting to activity checks;
- review operational updates.

Loss of PC access does not remove the requirement to notify a line manager or request an LOA where another device is available.

## 19. Support tickets

### Indicative response times

- General support initial response: around six hours where stated.
- Training & Recruitment: up to 48 hours.
- Human Resources: up to 48 hours.
- Public Relations: up to 48 hours.
- Ownership or Senior Management: up to 72 hours.
- General application review aim: up to 72 hours.

These are estimates, not guarantees.

### Empty tickets

A ticket without a stated question may be closed after six hours. It can normally be reopened by replying with a clear inquiry.

### Inactivity

Ticket systems may:

- send an inactivity reminder;
- mark the ticket inactive;
- issue a closure warning after 48 hours without a response;
- allow another 12 hours before closing;
- keep a resolved ticket open for a limited period.

The exact process depends on the ticket type.

### Senior Management requests

Support reviews requests for Senior Management before escalation.

Escalation may be appropriate for:

- matters Support cannot resolve;
- Ownership issues;
- serious policy concerns;
- appeals;
- major complaints;
- conflicts involving support staff;
- other genuinely senior matters.

Requesting a named owner or senior member does not automatically require escalation.

### Abuse of support staff

Abusive conduct may result in:

- a warning;
- ticket closure;
- a support ban;
- Senior Management review.

### Forwarded Discord messages

Forwarded messages may not be readable by the support system. Screenshots or image uploads should be provided instead.

## 20. Bloxlink verification and account changes

Bloxlink is used for Roblox-to-Discord verification and role updates. It is not the banning system.

### Changing Roblox account

The user should:

1. connect the intended Roblox account through the Bloxlink dashboard;
2. re-verify in each relevant TUI server;
3. use `/update` or `/verify` in the relevant bot-command channel.

The Roblox account used for TUI should be the one linked to the Discord account through Bloxlink.

### Changing Discord account

Staff may request:

- old Discord account;
- new Discord account;
- confirmation that the new account is Bloxlink-verified to the Roblox account.

TUI Support may restore TUI roles after verification. Technical problems with Bloxlink itself are handled through Bloxlink’s dashboard, tutorials, or support.
